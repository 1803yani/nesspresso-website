About Ness Presso

At Ness Presso Café, every cup tells a story — one brewed from passion, precision, and the perfect roast. Nestled in the heart of Poblacion, Bontoc, Southern Leyte, our café is a cozy hideaway for dreamers, creators, and coffee lovers alike.

From the smooth aroma of freshly ground beans to the comforting warmth of our ambiance, we bring people together over conversations and caffeine. We serve everything from handcrafted espresso to our signature Ness Latte — the perfect blend of art and flavor.

Step inside, slow down, and savor the moment. Because here at Ness Presso, coffee isn’t just a drink — it’s an experience.
